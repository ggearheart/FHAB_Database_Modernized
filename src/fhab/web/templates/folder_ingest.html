Ingest lab email folders

Drop in the files that arrived with a Bend Genetics / partner lab email — the wide-format results CSV plus any chain-of-custody, transmittal, and sample-receipt PDFs. The chemistry is converted to CEDEN long form and materialized as unlinked samples (geocoded from the CEDEN station registry where the station code is known), ready to reconcile on the workboard. The original files are kept on the batch.

Many folders at once

Have a parent folder containing many lab folders (one per email)? Select it and each subfolder is ingested as its own batch. Folders upload one at a time with progress below, so a large set (50+) won't overload a single request.
