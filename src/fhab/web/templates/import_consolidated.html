Import prepared CSV

Load a consolidated CEDEN-long CSV that already has geocoding and routine classification computed (e.g. the prepared Bend export). Each distinct SamplingEvent becomes its own sampling event (batch); samples are materialized with their Latitude/Longitude, tagged routine where SamplingType says so, and left unlinked otherwise for reconciliation on the workboard.

Expected columns: SamplingEvent, Region, StationCode, StationName, SampleDate, SampleTime, ProjectCode, LabBatch, BG_ID, LabSampleID, SampleType, Analyte, MethodName, Result, ResQualCode, Units, Fraction, MatrixName, Latitude, Longitude, SamplingType. Idempotent on BG_ID.
