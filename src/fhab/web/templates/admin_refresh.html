Refresh from data.ca.gov

Pull the latest published reports, cases, responses (+advisories), and results from the State Water Board's Freshwater HABs dataset and update the schema. This is an upsert: new records are inserted and the published fields of existing records are refreshed to match data.ca.gov.

  • Nothing is deleted — records here but not in the published files are left alone.
  • Local-only data is preserved — suspected illness, assignments, QA, lab links, and determinations are not touched; only published columns update.
  • Always Preview first to see what would change, then Apply.
